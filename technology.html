Location

I am fortunate to have spot in the corner of our backyard suitable for observing and astrophotography. Strictly speaking, we live within the city limits of Boulder, Colorado - but on the northwest corner of town. The view to southeast is essentially unusable due to the light dome of the Denver metropolitan area, but the view to the west and north is about 5 on the Bortle scale. Visually, I cannot do much more than observe planets, double stars, and bright star clusters. However, with modern imaging equipment and processing techniques, it is possible to produce the images you see on this site.
