veryoldphotons presents images of galaxies, nebulae, star clusters, comets, eclipses - all made by me from ancient starlight collected in my own suburban backyard.

You can enjoy your visit by just browsing the images. If you really want to geek out, use the toolbar on the lefthand edge of the display to explore each object in detail.
